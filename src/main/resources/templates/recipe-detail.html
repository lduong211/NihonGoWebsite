Ramen

Ramen has become an indispensable part of Japanese culinary culture. The most quintessential feature of the noodle bowl lies in the broth. A fragrant sweet broth is simmered from bones, pork, vegetables and other spices. Therefore, Ramen broth contains a lot of nutrients, especially collagen. Enjoying this dish, you will feel the sophistication of Japanese cuisine from the delicious chewy noodles blended in the rich broth and various side dishes.


  • 4 onion boaro
  • 1 onion
  • 1kg of pig bone
  • 1kg of pig bone
  • 1kg of bacon
  • 300ml Sake
  • 50ml Mirin alcohol
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 600ml dark soy sauce
  • 5 chicken eggs
  • Ramen noodles (You can buy them at supermarkets)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dried chili
  • Dried seaweed

Cut 3 baros. Cut the onion in half. Carrots, thick sliced ​​potatoes. Thinly sliced ​​ginger. Crushed garlic. Boil the bones of the pig's tube through boiling water. After that, pick it up and soak it in an ice bath. Next, you put boaro, onion, potato, carrot, ginger, garlic, dried chili, and pork bone in the pot with 4 liters of water.

+ Preliminary processing of meat Peel the bacon from the bacon. Slice the bacon in half. Then, curl up, then wrap the thread, fasten the meat. Next, marinate meat with salt and pepper. You bring the meat to sauté the golden brown and then boil for about 10 minutes. Then, remove the meat and soak it in a tub of cold ice water.

+ Making meat stock broth Boil 200ml Sake, 50ml Mirin wine, garlic, ginger, 100g brown sugar, 300ml dark soy sauce and 1 liter of water to dissolve ingredients and spices. Next, you put the meat in the warehouse for 45 minutes. Then, you take out the meat to cool, slice and store in the refrigerator.

Boil 5 eggs for about 8 minutes, then remove the shell. How to make soy sauce soaked eggs: put 300ml of water, 100ml of Sake, 100ml of dark soy sauce, 100g of brown sugar, and ginger in a pot to boil for the spices to dissolve evenly, then turn off the heat, let cool, let the eggs soak.

Boil water, add the noodles to the boil. Note that you do not let the noodles too soft to lose the inherent toughness. Boaro the white part you cut the thin thread. Thin sliced ​​green part. Then soak each type separately in a bowl of ice cold water. Put the noodles in the bowl, put the char siu meat, the egg soaked in soy sauce, cut in half, boaro on top and fill with the wheat broth, adding seaweed leaves to enjoy it!

You can buy ramen noodles at local supermarkets, local markets or Japanese food specialty stores. However, the simple way to make ramen noodles below will help you to cook a delicious and full bowl of noodles by yourself. + Ingredients: 500g flour, 150ml water and 5g refined salt + How to do it: mix salt with water, then pour slowly into the powder. Mix well until the dough is 1 block. If you don't have a spreader, you can add a little more water and then add the flour to a clean nylon bag. Then, use your feet to step up to knead the dough to save effort. Knead until the dough is cohesive, take the dough out of the bag, let the dough rest for 10 minutes and then use a knife to cut into small fibers. Note that you sprinkle a little more flour so that the noodles do not stick together. The Japanese enjoy ramen at any time of the day. When you eat, you should smoke hard to feel the length of the noodles and should not bite into different sections. Do not hesitate to make a noise when smoking because according to Japanese culture, the stronger the noodle is, the more delicious the bowl of noodles is, and the chef who cooks this dish has been successful. How to cook Japanese ramen is not too difficult, just follow the steps above to have an attractive dish to change your taste!
