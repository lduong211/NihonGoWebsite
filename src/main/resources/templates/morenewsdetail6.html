The 27-year-old beauty was crowned Miss Thailand

Natthapat Pongpraphan, 27 years old, won the crown of Miss Thailand in the final on December 13.

Natthapat Pongpraphan surpassed 29 contestants to win the final held at the International Convention and Exhibition Center in Chiang Mai. She received the crown from Miss Thailand 2019 Sireethorn Leearamwat.

The first runner-up belonged to Nitsthakan Aksornwan, second runner-up was Supannikar Nopparat, third runner-up was Kamonporn Thongphol and fourth runner-up was Patitta Santiwitch.

She is 27 years old this year, 1.73 m high, graduated in Geosciences, Mahidol University. The beauty will represent Thailand to participate in the Miss International pageant scheduled to take place in October 2021.

Natthapat Pongpraphan has extensive experience in beauty arenas. She was crowned Miss Grand Nakhon Pathom 2017, Miss CosmoWorld 2017, in the top 20 Miss Thailand Peace 2017. This year, she attended Miss Universe Thailand and stopped at the Top 20.

Miss Thailand is one of the oldest beauty contests in this country. Last year, the beautiful Sireethorn Leearamwat was crowned.
