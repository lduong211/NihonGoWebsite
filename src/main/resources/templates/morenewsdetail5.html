Short-term adjustable securities

Liquidity fell and the resistance level of 1,050 points made analysts concerned about the possibility of short-term correction.

At the end of last week (December 7-11), VN-Index and VN30 increased by 2.4% and 2.47%, marking the sixth consecutive week of gaining. VN-Index kept green in 3/5 sessions, stopped at nearly 1,046 points, the highest level since May 17, 2018 and increased by nearly 9% compared to the beginning of the year. In terms of liquidity, the average net matched value averaged approximately VND 10,000 billion per session, significantly higher than VND 6,200 billion per session in the first four months of 2018.

However, the sharp decline in liquidity in the last session of the week caused concerns about short-term prospects.

Last Friday, trading volume on HoSE decreased by 20% and 26.4% for the VN30. Notably, the trading volume on the VN30 index has fallen below the 50-day moving average.

SSI's report said that short-term unexpectedly correcting risks still exist. "The bullish momentum of the market is quite good when the VN-Index surged and surpassed the previous peak. However, the sharp decline in trading volume is a signal that the market is cautious as the VN-Index moves forward. near the resistance area of ​​1,050 points ", the report stated.

With the same point of view, KB Vietnam Securities Company also stated that the point to observe more at this time is that the trading volume is tending to decrease, although the index continues to surpass its peak. "If this does not improve in the next sessions, the VN-Index is likely to enter the knee correction soon, with greater pressure after the last beat", according to KB Vietnam expert.

From a more positive view, MB Securities Company (MBS) analyzed, the market liquidity cooled down but remained at a high level. Meanwhile, foreign investors kept their trading rhythm positive. Technically, MBS believes that the VN-Index has a potential to extend its upturn momentum towards the resistance zone of 1,064-1,080 points this week.

"The circulation of cash flow in leading stocks and pillars is a sign that the market is strong. Besides, the net buying of foreign investors in recent weeks may be possible. is considered to be the driving force for the market, "reported by MBS.

Recommendation for investment, SSI analyst group thinks that the nearest strong support area on the VN-Index is at 986-1,011 points. Investors might consider buying when the index dropped to near the above support level. On the contrary, when returning to the uptrend, the target price area of ​​the VN-Index after correcting at 1,138 points and 1,200 points respectively.

More cautious, KB Vietnam only recommends investors to avoid chasing buying, waiting for clearer corrections before reopening short-term positions.
