Passengers 'criticism' of flights on Tet holidays?

2021-01-17: 3 hours ago

The Civil Aviation Authority of Vietnam said: It is expected that during the 2021 Lunar New Year period from January 27 to February 26 (ie December 15 to January 15 of the lunar calendar), airlines will operate on average more than 1,000 domestic flights. / day, up 28% over the same period last year. Peak day reached 1,200 flights / day, up 25.3%.

In addition to the flights in the daytime, Vietnamese airlines will increase the operating load on the night time from 10:00 am-7:00 the next day with an average total of 140-150 flights. /day.

However, according to information from the Airport Corporation (ACV), passenger demand for flights after 23:00 peak Lunar New Year is very low.

Specifically, according to the scheduled flight increase schedule, the peak period of Lunar New Year 2021 of domestic airlines, airlines have many flights after 23 hours at airports with operating time from 6 to 21. or 23 o'clock is Tho Xuan, Dong Hoi, Phu Cat, Chu Lai, Tuy Hoa.

Statistics on the peak occasion of the Lunar New Year 2020 show that: At Dong Hoi Port, after 23:00, only Bamboo Airways operates 18 flights, accounting for 1.69% of flights at the port; Pleiku port has Jetstar Pacific (now Pacific Airlines) operating 1 flight, accounting for 0.09%; Tho Xuan port is slightly higher, with a total of 10 flights after 23 hours, including: VietJet Air 5 flights, Vietnam Airlines 3 flights, Jetstar Pacific 2 flights, accounting for 0.43% of flights; Chu Lai Port serves 6 flights of Jetstar Pacific, accounting for 0.32% and finally Phu Cat port has 9 flights after 23 hours including: VietJet Air 4 flights, Bamboo Airways 4 flights, Jetstar Pacific 1 flight accounting for 0.36% of flights operating at ports.

Thus, a total of 5 above ports operate only 44 out of 2,218 flights after 23 hours during the Lunar New Year 2020 season, reaching a rate of 0.5%, although the night flight fare is often significantly lower than flights. daytime.

The representative of ACV added: This unit has made great efforts to invest and upgrade the infrastructure system, equipment, technical systems, human resources to ensure good service during peak times. ensure absolute security, safety, quality of passenger service in the peak period of Tet. The total capacity of aircraft parking lots at airports reaches 360 parking lots. In which, the two ports with the highest demand for moving, Tan Son Nhat and Noi Bai ports, are arranged 118 and 90 parking positions respectively, meeting the needs of airlines' aprons.
