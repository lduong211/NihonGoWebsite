Pho

To get a good bowl of pho depends a lot on the experience and know-how of the processor, especially the pot of broth. "Genuine beef oyster pho must be very sophisticated, but everything we do is simple and gentle, still feel delicious" - housewife My Na shares her home noodle recipe with a secret below.


  • Beef / pig bone (preferably beef bone for standard): 1kg
  • Beef: 500 gr
  • Noodles: 1kg
  • Cardamom: 2 results
  • Ginger: small branch (80gr)
  • Episode: 2 pieces
  • Cinnamon: a branch
  • Dried onion: 2 bulbs
  • Onions: 1/2 bulb
  • Scallions, Chinese herbs, coriander, fresh chili, rock sugar, monosodium glutamate, salt, seasoning seeds

Wash the bones and simmer with a little salt. If it is beef bone, it should be baked through the bone, then boiled, poured the first water and then simmered. Often at home, mothers will do it quickly, so it doesn't last long. If you have prepared in advance, everyone should simmer bones for at least 6 hours (maximum 12 hours) for a very delicious broth. Can be made from the night before, to simmer the bones to have a pot of delicious broth the next day.

Ginger, dried onions, cinnamon, anise, cardamom and then crushed, all put in a pot of bone broth. When close, add 1 onion to the pot and cook. When the broth is finished, add seasoning seeds to taste (pay attention to remove foam during the setting process)

Cut beef to taste. Wash onions, Chinese coriander, cilantro / basil, cut it short. The noodle cake is blanched over boiling water.

All placed in bowls, filled with broth, added lemons, chili to have a bowl of genuine pho at home. Note that the broth must be boiled for the beef to cook. Fragrant and sweet! Add a few flips to standard Hanoi beef noodle soup.

Actually making pho only takes a lot of work to make broth. So each time you simmer the broth, to save your time, everyone should simmer a lot and let the broth thicken a bit. Every time you need to cook noodles and pho, you will add water and prepare other ingredients quickly, you will have a hot noodle dish for the whole family! It's convenient and delicious
