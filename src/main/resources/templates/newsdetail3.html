Industry leading AI experts converge at AI4VN 2020

Experts in big data, machine learning, computer vision ... will share experiences in research and development and technical solutions for AI.

AI4VN 2020 opens this morning at 27/11 at the Convention Center 272 Vo Thi Sau, Ward 7, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City with the participation of hundreds of experts, managers, trainers, entrepreneurs ... in the field of artificial intelligence (AI) domestically and internationally.

In which, Prof. Yoshua Benjo , Founding Member of Element AI, Canana will have a presentation on the approach to AI training in developing countries and advice for Vietnamese human resources.

Prof. Yoshua Benjo. Photo: NVCC.

Prof. Yoshua Benjo has many years of research in computer science, winning major awards in the world. He will give suggestions to offset the talent shortage in the AI ​​field and approach knowledge of AI with developing countries like Vietnam.

Also talking about AI research and development experience, Dr. Bui Hai Hung , Institute of Artificial Intelligence Research, Vingroup will share from the actual example where he is working.

Dr. Bui Hai Hung. Photo: NVCC.

Field of research of Dr. Hung including graphical model probability (probabilistic graphical models), the fertility pattern deep (deep generative models), inference Bayesian (Bayesian inference) and machine learning (machine learning), especially model for data as strings and relational data (models for sequential and relational data) application to identify the action / behavioral tendencies of man, recognizable video (video understanding) and natural language processing (natural language processing).

Before returning to work, Dr. Bui Hai Hung was a researcher at Google DeepMind, Adobe Research and the Nuance Natural Language Understanding Lab (Nuance Natural Language Understanding Lab).

Dr. Do Trung Thong. Photo: NVCC.

During the session on AI Ecosystem in Ho Chi Minh City, Dr. Do Trung Thong will share about some applications of AI technology. Dr. Thong has 5 patents in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence.

Dr. Thong is co-founder and CEO of Palexy - a technology company in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence - that provides analytical technology that allows retailers to measure customer behavior and performance. of employee.

Prof. Vu Ha Van. Photo: NVCC.

Prof. Vu Ha Van , Scientific Director, VinBigdata Big Data Research Institute, Vingroup Group, will have an article on Open Data Warehouse for AI.

Joining Vingroup in 2018, as Scientific Director, Professor Van was one of the first people to lay the foundation for the formation and development of VinBigdata Big Data Research Institute; aiming to become the leading Vietnam Data Science and Technology Research Center, world-class.

Associate Professor, Dr. Thoai Nam.

From the Polytechnic University, National University of Ho Chi Minh City, Assoc.Prof. Dr. Thoai Nam will talk about the computational infrastructure system for HPC-AI-Bigdata and the prospect of quantum computing.

He received his doctorate from the Johannes Kepler University of Linz - Austria. He is the founder and Head of the Polytechnic University's High Performance Computing Lab. Areas of research include high-performance computing, high-performance data analysis and artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and network protocol.

Mr. Le Hong Viet. Photo: NVCC.

Coming from a technology research and development enterprise, Mr. Le Hong Viet , General Director of FPT Smart Cloud Company, shared about the new normal AI at FPT .

Joining FPT since 2005, Mr. Viet has held many important positions, contributing to strategic technology planning, serving production and business activities of the corporation. Mr. Viet is one of the people who laid the foundation for the application and development of technologies that help FPT conquer international markets such as the US, Japan, France, Singapore ... in many different areas such as AI, Cloud Computing , Big Data, IoT ...

Associate Professor Vu Hai Quan. Photo: NVCC.

Assoc.Prof. Dr. Vu Hai Quan , Deputy Director of the National University of Ho Chi Minh City will deliver a presentation in the opening session. Mr. Quan obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Trento University, Italy.

The fields of research he pursues include: Sound modeling, languages ​​in Continuous Large Vocabulary Recognition; Corpus linguistic development (sound and text); Music and sound restoration; Translate speech ...

The annual Vietnam Artificial Intelligence Festival (AI4VN) is expected to be a driving force for AI technology in Vietnam, through connecting components inside the ecosystem from research institutes and universities. learning, business, technology corporation, startup to the AI ​​community.

The goal of AI technology research and application is aimed at many key areas of Vietnam such as health, education, commerce, finance, agriculture ...

The AI4VN 2020 program is organized by the Ministry of Science and Technology, the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the People's Committee of Ho Chi Minh City, and VnExpress online newspaper, with the companionship of sponsors Vietinbank, VinBigdata, FPT, VIB, Phenikaa.
