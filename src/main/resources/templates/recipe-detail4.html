Com Tam

Com tam is a famous delicacy originating from Saigon land. Let's learn how to make com tam with this standard Saigon com tam recipe!


  • 1 kg of lean shoulder meat, lean ham or bacon.
  • 4 garlic cloves.
  • 2 purple onions.
  • 3 white scallion heads.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper.
  • 2 tablespoons oyster oil.
  • 0.5 tbsp of sesame oil.
  • 2 tablespoons delicious fish sauce.
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce.
  • 0.5 tbsp of seasoning seeds.
  • 1 tablespoon honey. 2 tablespoons condensed milk. 0.5 teaspoons of colored water.
  • 1 pinch of five flavors. Just a little to not stop the smell of grilled meat characteristic of eating com tam.
  • 1 little ground pepper.
  • 1 tablespoon of oil.

Step 1: The meat is washed, sliced ​​not too thick. Using a knife handle or hammer to slam the meat, gradually evenly onto both sides of the meat (This way, when grilled, it will be soft, but also have a bigger, more attractive look).

Step 2: Top green onion + garlic + chopped purple onion. Put it all in a blender or puree.

Step 3: Put the mixture of ground onion garlic (step 2) into the bowl with the remaining spices above and mix well (leave cooking oil). Finally, put the meat in a two-sided marinade. Remember, after 15 minutes add oil to the meat. Cover the meat, put the meat in the refrigerator cooler overnight or at least 4-5 hours before baking.

Step 4: Meat is arranged on the blister. The oven preheats 15 minutes at a temperature of 180 - 200 degrees C. Put the meat griddle in the oven for 20-22 minutes or faster depending on the temperature of the oven (coal fire is better). When the flesh is slightly discolored, use a brush to brush the marinade over the meat. Turn the meat when you see it burning next to it, turn it over and make a wall for the other side. When the meat is burned next to the golden edge, turn off the oven to remove the meat blister.

When preparing the marinade, if you want to be sure, taste it after adding all the marinade to the taste before adding the meat. Family decrease depending on family taste.

All ingredients are mixed and beaten and then steamed. To check whether the pie is cooked or not, we use a toothpick to stab it. If the toothpick doesn't stick, the pie is ripe.

2 crushed yolks with a little water. Take this mixture and sweep it on the surface of the sausage after steaming and then bring it to a 180-200 degree C oven for 5-7 minutes. Orange-yellow rolls are satisfactory.

Fish sauce with com tam: Ratio of 4 or 5 fish sauce: 2 sugar: 1 boiling water, minced garlic and chili depending on taste.

Serve hot rice, egg rolls, ribs with chili garlic fish sauce and a little sour food to balance the flavor. You can add spring rolls, omelets if you want.

The weather is getting cold like this and enjoying a delicious dish like com tam, maybe nothing more "pleasing to the stomach"?
