Gasoline and oil prices rose sharply

From 15:30, each liter of E5RON92 gasoline increased by VND 609, RON95 III increased by VND 650, and oil products increased by VND 576-651...

The Ministry of Industry and Trade - Finance said, with this adjustment, the maximum price of E5RON92 gasoline reached 14,494 VND while the highest price of RON95 gasoline was 15,351 VND. Diesel price increased by 596 VND, to 11,434 VND per liter. Kerosene increased by 576 VND per liter to 10,138 VND. The maximum madut oil is 11,742 VND / kg, equivalent to an increase of 651 VND.

To curb the increase, the operator continues to use tools from the Stabilization Fund. This time, the Inter-ministry did not deduct funds for E5RON92 gasoline, kerosene and madut oil. RON95 gasoline is deducted 100 VND per liter, equivalent to the previous operating period. Meanwhile, the fund deduction of diesel oil decreased to 100 dong per liter, the previous period was 200 dong.

The executive agency also spent 1,000 dong from the compensation fund for each liter of E5RON92 gasoline, the previous period was 900 dong. The cost of RON95 is 200 dong per liter, in the previous period it was 100 dong, madut was 200 dong per kilo (the previous period was 100 dong), diesel and kerosene was at 300 dong per liter.

The Ministry of Industry and Trade - Finance said that if they do not continue to spend the stabilization fund, the price of petroleum will increase from the current price of about 850-1,600 VND per liter.
